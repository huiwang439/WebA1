Consumption demand

Office workers usually need coffee to wake up. After a long workday, especially when they arrive at the office in the morning or feel sleepy in the afternoon, a cup of coffee can help them recover quickly and improve their work efficiency.

Coffee is also a social drink. During breaks from work, colleagues may go to a coffee shop near the office to chat, discuss work or relax. Not only is this a way to socialize, but it also helps to strengthen team cohesion.

Consumption habit

They are more likely to buy coffee that is convenient and fast. Some office workers may opt to join a coffee subscription service and receive fresh coffee beans or ground coffee on a regular basis.

There are certain requirements for the quality and taste of coffee. They may choose different sources of coffee beans, degrees of roasting and methods of coffee preparation depending on their preferences.
