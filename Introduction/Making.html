1. Drip Filter: Using gravity to let hot water pass through the layer of coffee powder, slowly drip filtering out the coffee liquid, suitable for daily drinking, with a balanced taste.
2. French Press: extracting coffee through immersion and physical pressure, which can fully release the oils and flavors of coffee, with a rich and mellow taste.
3. Espresso: high-pressure rapid extraction of finely ground coffee powder, to get highly concentrated coffee liquid, strong taste, rich aroma, is the basis for making a variety of coffee drinks.
4. Cold extraction: low temperature and long time soaking coarse ground coffee powder, the extracted coffee has a soft taste, low acidity and obvious sweetness, suitable for summer drinking.
5. Hand brewing: manually extracting coffee through fine control of water temperature, water flow rate and water injection in a circle, etc. It can highlight the original flavor of coffee beans and is suitable for coffee lovers to explore different flavors.

Cold Brew

Coffee powder + water immersion

Mode: Immersion extraction

Taste: Bitter with low acidity

Time: 8-24h

Ice Americano

Concentrate + water + ice

Method: High-pressure extraction in an espresso machine

Sensation: Highly mellow with smoky flavor

Time: 30s

Ice Drip

Ice water dripping into the ground coffee

Mode: Extraction

Taste: outstanding sweetness and sourness, with a strong sense of layering

Time: after 4-8h of iced drip, continue to refrigerate for 24h

Ice Hand Brewed

Hot water brewing coffee + ice cubes

Mode: Drip Filter Extraction

Taste: Outstanding sweetness and sourness, strong layers of flavor

Time: 2-2.5mins
