Sun-drying

Sun-drying, the most traditional and long-established process, is also known as natural drying or simply drying.

The beans produced by this method are less acidic and sweeter, and have a complex and rich flavor, although they are not as clean as they could be.

The Washing Process

The washed method, as the classic treatment method is called, means exactly what it says - the beans are washed with water.

This method results in a coffee with fresh and bright acidity, a pure and unadulterated taste, and a refreshing flavor, but its richness is relatively weak.

Honey Treatment

Originating from a unique Costa Rican process, this treatment skillfully combines the advantages of the solarization and washing methods.

It is characterized by an outstanding flavor, sweet and sour, with an aromatic and mellow texture.
