Method

Sun-drying, the most traditional and long-established process, is also known as natural drying or simply drying.

The washed method, as the classic treatment method is called, means exactly what it says - the beans are washed with water.

Originating from a unique Costa Rican process, this treatment skillfully combines the advantages of the solarization and washing methods.
