Consumption demand

Students need coffee to stay awake and focused while studying, especially during test preparation. For example, college students often rely on coffee to stay up late during final exam week or when preparing for major exams such as postgraduate or public exams.

Coffee also plays an important role in campus socializing. Students may drink coffee during recess or at a coffee shop on campus to meet with classmates, discuss group assignments or participate in community activities.

Consumption habit

Students' spending power is relatively limited, so they may choose more cost-effective coffee. For example, affordable coffee served in the school cafeteria, or some brands of instant coffee. However, with the improvement of living standards, there are also many students willing to occasionally consume some specialty coffee.

Students are more susceptible to fashion trends. If a coffee drink becomes popular on social media, such as the recent raw coconut latte, it will become an instant hit with the student population and everyone will want to try it.
