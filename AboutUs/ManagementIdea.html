ABOUT US

  • Audience Group

  • Social Responsibility

We believe that coffee is not only a drink, but also a culture and way of life. Therefore, we offer the service of DIY coffee beans in store, so that customers can personally participate in the coffee making process. In this process, customers can understand the source of coffee beans, roasting principles and other knowledge, increase the understanding and love of coffee. For example, after experiencing fried coffee beans, many customers have a deeper understanding of the taste and flavor of coffee, and also cherish the coffee they make.

We also focus on personalization. Customers can take photos on the spot or provide their own photos on the wrapping paper to get their own brand coffee. This personalized service can meet the unique needs of customers and make them feel a different consumption experience. Whether given as a gift to a friend or enjoyed by yourself, this coffee with a personal stamp has a special meaning.

In addition, we encourage interaction and communication among our customers. Customers can make more coffee to stay in the store, and can come to the store to enjoy it later, or they can authorize some coffee beans to exchange with other customers. This interaction not only increases the connection between customers, but also creates a unique coffee community atmosphere.
